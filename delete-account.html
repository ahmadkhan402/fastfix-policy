Delete Account – FastFix Partner

If you would like to delete your FastFix Partner account and associated data, please follow one of the methods below.

Option 1: Delete from App

  1. Open the FastFix Partner app
  2. Go to Account
  3. Tap on "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm your request

Option 2: Request via Email

You can request account deletion by contacting us at:

fastfixmanage@gmail.com

Data Deletion Details

  • Your personal information (name, phone number, profile data) will be permanently deleted
  • Uploaded images and activity data will be removed
  • Some data may be retained temporarily for legal or security purposes
⚠️ Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone.

For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.
