FastFix-Partner uses your information for the following purposes:

FastFix-Partner does not sell your personal data. We only share your information in these limited circumstances:

FastFix-Partner implements industry-standard security measures, including encryption (HTTPS), to protect your personal data and identity documents during transmission and storage. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

FastFix-Partner retains your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations.
